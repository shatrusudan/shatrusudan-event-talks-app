LATEST WORLD NEWS - JUNE 16, 2026

1. G7 Summit Convened in Évian-les-Bains, France
World leaders gathered in Évian-les-Bains, France for the annual G7 summit to discuss key global issues and international conflicts. President Donald Trump attended, showcasing a recently signed preliminary peace agreement between the United States and Iran. The summit's focus subsequently transitioned to discussions regarding the ongoing war in Ukraine.

2. U.S.-Iran Preliminary Peace Agreement & Middle East Tensions
The United States and Iran signed a preliminary peace agreement aiming to reopen the Strait of Hormuz and implement a 60-day ceasefire. However, the agreement is facing immediate strain. Iranian officials accused Israel of violating the agreement through military operations in Lebanon, as Israel continued its airstrikes in southern Lebanon.

3. Russian Warning Shots in the English Channel & Fuel Limits
In maritime developments, the Russian frigate Admiral Grigorovich reportedly fired warning shots at a yacht in the English Channel. Within Russia, domestic energy supply constraints prompted fuel company Tatneft to introduce temporary caps on gasoline and diesel sales, demanding cash payments at various stations.

4. UK Government Plans Social Media Ban for Under-16s
The government led by British Prime Minister Keir Starmer announced plans to prohibit children under the age of 16 from using ten major social media platforms starting next year.

5. Earthquake in Palu, Indonesia
A magnitude 6.7 earthquake struck the city of Palu in Central Sulawesi, Indonesia. The disaster resulted in at least one death and caused injuries to dozens of people.

6. Bank of Japan Raises Interest Rates to 1%
In economic news, the Bank of Japan increased its benchmark interest rate to 1%, marking its highest rate level since 1995.

7. Study Highlights Role of Indigenous Stewardship in Climate Fight
A new study published in Nature Humanities and Social Sciences Communications highlighted that utilizing Indigenous knowledge and stewardship is crucial for preserving global biodiversity and mitigating the impacts of climate change.
